About
My name is Matthew Agius and I am a 21 year old student from Cary, North Carolina. I am currently attending the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I have a passion for computer programming, and I plan to achieve a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Cyber Security and a Minor in Bioinformatics and Genomics.
In my first year of college I achieved a 3.8 GPA and was placed on The Chancellor's List as a result. Later that year I won the Unisys Corporation Academic Scholarship and I became a member of the 49th Security Division Cyber Security Club.
Recently, I completed my second intership at Fidelity Investments. During my first internship with Fidelity I worked with the Personal Investing Cyber Security Team where I designed web tools to be used in the development process. During my second internship at Fidelity I worked with a front end web development team to create web applications using the Agile Methodology. During this time I was exposed to the web development life cycle and many of the tools used in modern web development.
During High School, I was a member of the Black Falcons Cyber Security Team at Green Hope High School. My team members and I competed in National Cyber Security Competitions against teams from all over the country. We even used a flying video drone to live stream our football team's Homecoming game. (USA Today Article)(Triangle Business Journal Article)
I also ran Cross Country for all 4 years of high school where I was a team captain. I received the Coaches Award on my senior year.
I gained experience volunteering with the Y.M.C.A. with their Y-Guides and Princesses program. I have been volunteering for the past 7 years at their various summer camps in North Carolina.
